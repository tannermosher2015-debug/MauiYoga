Your first morning

What to expect.

01

Just come as you are

No special clothing, no flexibility required. Bring a mat if you have one; we can lend one if you don’t.

02

Tell her your body

Share any injuries, conditions, or worries beforehand. It’s exactly the information she’s trained to use.

03

Go at your pace

You’ll learn a little at a time. No one will push you past what’s safe for you today.
